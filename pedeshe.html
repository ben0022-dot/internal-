About Hon. Pedeshe George

A dedicated public servant committed to transforming Harambee Ward through economic empowerment, education, and infrastructure development. With years of experience in community organizing and policy advocacy, Hon. Pedeshe represents the voice of the people.

Key Achievements

  1. Education: Established 5 new schools and scholarship programs
  2. Healthcare: Built 3 community health centers
  3. Youth: Created 2,000+ jobs through NEDP programs
  4. Infrastructure: Paved 15km of roads and installed street lighting
  5. Women: Empowered 500+ women through microfinance
