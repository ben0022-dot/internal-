Our Themes & Agendas

Education Reform

Investing in quality education for all, building modern schools, and providing scholarships for bright students in Harambee Ward.

Youth Employment

Creating job opportunities through skills training, entrepreneurship programs, and partnerships with local industries.

Healthcare Access

Improving healthcare infrastructure, ensuring every resident has access to affordable and quality medical services.

Infrastructure

Building better roads, drainage systems, and public facilities to enhance the quality of life for all residents.

Environment

Promoting green initiatives, waste management, and sustainable practices for a cleaner Harambee Ward.

IEBC Voter Registration

Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission

The IEBC is the constitutional body responsible for conducting or supervising referenda and elections to any elective body or office established by the Constitution of Kenya.

Why Register?

  • Exercise your constitutional right to vote
  • Choose leaders who represent your values
  • Hold elected officials accountable
  • Shape the future of Harambee Ward
  • Participate in democratic governance

Requirements: Be a Kenyan citizen, 18 years or older, with a valid National ID or Passport.
