About Ben & Sharon

Founded in 2020, Ben & Sharon is a community-driven environmental platform that transforms local conservation efforts into measurable, gamified impact. We believe that when communities unite for nature, every small action creates ripples of lasting change.

Available Services

  1. Community Cleanup Coordination

    Organize and track neighborhood cleanup events with real-time participant tracking

  2. Tree Planting Initiatives

    Seasonal planting programs with species selection guidance and growth tracking

  3. River & Waterway Protection

    Plastic diversion programs and water quality monitoring networks

    Personal and community carbon offset calculators with reduction challenges
